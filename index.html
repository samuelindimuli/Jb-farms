About Us

Our Story

JB Farms was born from a simple but meaningful idea—honoring the legacy of two inspiring individuals, James and Beatrice.
Their passion for hard work, family, and self-sustainability laid the foundation for what JB Farms is today.
The name JB Farms represents more than just initials—it reflects a commitment to values passed down through generations: dedication, care, and a deep connection to the land.

What We Do

At JB Farms, we proudly raise a variety of animals including chickens, rabbits, guinea pigs, and cattle, ensuring they are well cared for in a healthy environment.
Alongside our livestock, we cultivate fresh produce in our greenhouse, where we grow tomatoes, as well as maintain a small but growing dragon fruit garden.
Our approach combines traditional farming values with modern techniques to ensure quality, sustainability, and freshness in everything we produce.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality farm products while building a trusted relationship with our community.
We aim to promote sustainable farming practices and deliver products that we can rely on as a family.
